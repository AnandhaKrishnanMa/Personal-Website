About me

Hello! I'm Anandha Krishnan, a passionate MERN stack developer currently honing my skills at the BootCamp known as Brototype. With a background in computer science and a penchant for problem-solving, I thrive in the dynamic world of web development.

My journey into the realm of coding began with a fascination for technology and a desire to create impactful solutions.
